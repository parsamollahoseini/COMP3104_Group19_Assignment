Importance of DevOps in Software Development Life Cycle

SDLC has a traditional phase of planning, development, testing, deployment and maintenance.

DevOps changes that by integrating development and integration throughout every SDLC stages, 
promoting automation, collaboration, and continuous feedback.

With that learning DevOps and its importance in SDLC would be very beneficial to any aspiring software engineers as 
you would learn to automate, collaborate and provide continous feedback in each step of the SDLC stage.

Key reasons why DevOps is important in SDLC
- Continuous Integration & Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)
- Automation Across the Lifecycle
- Improved Collaboration
- Early Bug Detection
- Faster Feedback Loops
- Better Security and Stability
